# nook

Nook is a social platform where people discover, collect, and combine inspiring spaces to create rooms that feel uniquely their own.

Instead of switching between Pinterest, TikTok, Amazon, IKEA, and measuring tape, Nook brings inspiration, shopping, and room planning into one interactive experience.

## Vision

Everyone deserves a space that feels like home, whether it's a college dorm, first apartment, or forever home.

Nook helps people discover spaces they love, understand why they work, and adapt them to fit their own rooms.

## MVP

- Browse inspiring rooms
- Explore rooms in interactive 3D
- Click products to view details
- Save inspiring "Nooks"
- Build your own room from multiple Nooks

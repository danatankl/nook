# Design Principles

## Inspiration over editing

Nook exists to help users discover ideas.

Editing tools should never overshadow inspiration.

---

## Personalization over copying

Users don't duplicate rooms.

They build their own.

---

## The room is the hero

UI should never distract from the room.

---

## Every object tells a story

Furniture isn't just an item.

It should explain why it belongs.

---

## Small spaces matter

Nooks are the building blocks of great rooms.
